### Growing Importance of Dynamic Cybersecurity Frameworks

Moving Target Defense (MTD) is transforming cybersecurity by continuously altering system configurations, network structures, and attack surfaces, making it significantly harder for cybercriminals to exploit vulnerabilities. Unlike traditional static security systems, MTD introduces unpredictability that disrupts reconnaissance activities and limits the success of zero-day attacks. Industries handling sensitive operational data are increasingly integrating adaptive security architectures to reduce exposure to evolving cyber threats.
